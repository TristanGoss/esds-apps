Rapid Membership card scanner

You can use this page to rapidly validate a large number of membership cards.

Simply tap the page once, then aim your camera at a card and watch / listen for the response.

You don't have to reload the page to scan multiple cards. Just aim your camera at them one after another.

If this page is not working reliably, please do not repeatedly tap or refresh the page. Instead, ensure you have a stable internet connection.
