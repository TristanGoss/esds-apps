Many thanks for choosing to purchase a membership with us! Your membership is valid until the end of the calendar year. We're very happy for you to use this digital membership card, but if you'd like a physical one as well, please email info@esds.org.uk to request one; We'll have one prepared for you to collect on one of our regular Thursdays within a week or two. We're also happy for you to print this card off in any medium you wish, provided the card can still be read by both human and machine.

Volunteering

ESDS is entirely run by volunteers, and we are always looking for new volunteers to join us on class and event nights.

Volunteering can take many forms including DJs, Teachers, Event Staff, Door Staff, and many more. Anyone volunteering does not have to pay on nights they volunteer!

If you are interested in learning more, please email info@esds.org.uk and a member of the committee be in touch.
