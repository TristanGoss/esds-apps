Many thanks for choosing to purchase a membership with us! Your membership is valid until the end of the calendar year. It entitles you to vote in our Annual General Meeting, and to use the concession rate when booking tickets for our events on Dancecloud.

If you're not able to use Dancecloud for a particular event, don't worry! You can bring your membership card (attached and shown below) with you and show it to our volunteers on the door. They will scan the QR code to verify your membership, and then charge you the concession rate. Please also bring your card along to the Annual General Meeting, so we can quickly tally members in attendance.

We would prefer for you to use this digital membership card, but if you'd like a physical one as well, please email info@esds.org.uk to request one; We'll have one prepared for you to collect on one of our regular Thursdays within a week or two. We're also happy for you to print this card off in any medium you wish, provided the card can still be read by both human and machine.

Volunteering

ESDS is entirely run by volunteers, and we are always looking for new volunteers to join us on class and event nights.

Volunteering can take many forms including DJs, Teachers, Event Staff, Door Staff, and many more. Anyone volunteering does not have to pay on nights they volunteer!

If you are interested in learning more, please email info@esds.org.uk and a member of the committee be in touch.
